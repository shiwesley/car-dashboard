The balance view

42 sensible, CarPlay-equipped cars scored 1–10 on nine traits, blended into one Overall Score — the highlighted green column right after the car names — using weights you control. Move a slider — the whole board re-sorts. Click any column heading to sort by it (again to flip low↔high). Under each car name, tap Ext, Int, or Green to see real photos on Google. Scores are directional and made to be edited.
